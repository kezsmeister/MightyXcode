## Description (4000 characters max)

Mighty helps busy parents organize and track their children's activities, classes, and schedules — all in one beautiful app.

**MANAGE MULTIPLE KIDS**
Create separate profiles for each child with their own schedules, activities, and progress tracking. Switch between kids with a single tap.

**FAMILY AGENDA VIEW**
See all your children's activities in one unified calendar. Color-coded by child so you'll never double-book or miss an important event.

**TRACK ACTIVITIES & CLASSES**
• Soccer practice, ballet, piano lessons — track them all
• Add times, notes, and photos to each activity
• Set reminders so you're never late

**RECURRING SCHEDULES**
Set up weekly activities once and Mighty handles the rest. Daily, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly — your schedule, your way.

**SYNC ACROSS DEVICES**
Your family's schedule syncs automatically across all your devices. Update on your iPhone, see it instantly on your iPad.

**SIMPLE & BEAUTIFUL**
Designed with busy parents in mind. No clutter, no complexity — just the features you need to keep your family organized.

• Tracking extracurricular activities
• Coordinating between caregivers
• Keeping memories with activity photos

Download Mighty today and take control of your family's busy schedule.
