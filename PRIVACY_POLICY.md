# Privacy Policy for Mighty

**Last Updated: January 16, 2026**

## Introduction

Mighty ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email Address**: Used solely for account authentication via secure magic link sign-in. We do not use your email for marketing purposes.

### Profile Information
- **Child Profile Names**: Names you choose to identify your children's profiles within the app.
- **Profile Avatars**: Emoji selections for profile customization.

### Activity Data
- **Activity Entries**: Titles, dates, times, and notes for scheduled activities.
- **Photos**: Images you choose to attach to activity entries (stored locally and in cloud sync).

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Identifiers**: For push notification delivery only.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:
- Authenticate your account securely
- Store and sync your activity data across devices
- Send push notification reminders for scheduled activities
- Provide the core functionality of the app

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using InstantDB cloud services
- Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
- We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
- Photos are stored securely and only accessible to your account

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us

## Children's Privacy

Mighty is designed for parents to manage their children's activities. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. All data is entered and managed by the parent/guardian account holder.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **InstantDB**: For secure cloud data storage and synchronization
- **Apple Push Notification Service**: For activity reminders

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
